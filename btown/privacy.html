B-Town Privacy Policy

Not much legal jargon here, just nice and simple terms about the information collected and used about you in B-Town.

 

The Information We Collect

We don't collect any information.

Depending on the social media network you use, you should read their privacy policy as B-Town only integrates them.

Your user profile helps you customize your account and share the tours and images you like. Your profile in B-Town includes your username and profile image. Once you log out, that information is deleted and only retreived when you log back in.

Rating are sent anonymously to the server and are only used to establish which tours are most liked. This will allow us to tailor future tours in the preferred style.

 

How We Use and Disclose Your Information

Quite simply, we don't disclose any information. B-Town only uses your profile name and picture to allow you to see you have logged in with certain social media.

The profile is used to grant permission to the tours data. The tour data is saved and updated when new tours become available.

 

Changes to This Privacy Policy

As B-Town develops, we may decide to revise this privacy policy from time to time. The most current version will govern our use of your information and will always be at privacy, and older versions are available upon request. If we make changes that we think will substantially alter your rights, we will notify you by posting a notice on our website. You agree to accept any changes or revisions to this privacy policy by continuing to use B-Town.
