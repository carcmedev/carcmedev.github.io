What is it?

Moustache is a small, simple and free call simulator that offers a number of features to get you out of that unproductive meeting or those awkward situations we all too often find ourselves in.

The App

Small, simple and free call simulator.

Discrete Mode

Activate the call with a simple press of the volume and attract no attention.

Simple Interface

No setup is required, Moustache will work straight from the Google Play Store

Personalize

Personalized your call. Select your contact, the ring tone and when the incoming call will occur. Any image associated with the contact will be displayed with the call
